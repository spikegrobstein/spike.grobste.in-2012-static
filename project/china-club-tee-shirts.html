China Club Tee Shirts

Categories

  • Artwork Recreation
  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
  • Screenprinting

The China Club in New York City needed tee shirts done but the only artwork they had available for their logo was on their flash-based site. A low-resolution bitmap graphic.

I had to redraw the art in Illustrator to create a vector version for screenprinting at 12".

  • 12" 5-color screenprinting on black tee shirts.
  • Adobe Illustrator for art.
  • Adobe Photoshop for halftone glow.
